Sam's story

Justin's story

Sam caught Justin's eye first because she was cute. And then he noticed she was shy, but friendly. Maybe a little aloof, in her own world. She did not care what those around her thought of her, and was never trying to impress anyone. Justin was impressed.

Thankfully, their Adult Recess sports league made it easy to mingle after an hour of freeze tag. Slow to make any moves, and even slower to not startle a shy person, Justin had his mind made up to be her friend. If nothing else, this person would make a good buddy. He sensed they were similar.

Sam sensed no such thing. Justin was but a maybe-friend.

Justin and Sam ended up on a group ski trip together, mostly because of their mutual friendship with Lisa, the most friendly person in the sports league.

Justin hoped he could spark a friendship with this fun new person. And Sam was excited to make new friends, as she was still a fresh face in the city.

Justin's intuitions about the new cute person were confirmed. She was fun, carefree, and liked being silly. Similar! But even more special, uniquely Sam qualities emerged, like how it didn't seem to alarm her that the car might crash if we took it down the icy road too fast. As if there were no preference at all for crash or no crash. Or, like when she fell down the stairs and hurt herself, but you'd never know because she was laughing too hard.
